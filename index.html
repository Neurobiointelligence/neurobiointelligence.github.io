About

We study the principles of Neurobiological Intelligence by investigating neural dynamics of cell types and circuits. Our lab integrates behavioral paradigms, imaging, optogenetics, molecular biology, and computational modeling to reveal how motivation, memory, and behavior interact at the circuit level.
